# Chilean Government Liabilities: Official Public Data Sources

## Comprehensive Research — March 2026

---

## 1. DIPRES (Direccion de Presupuestos / Budget Directorate)

DIPRES is the single most important source for consolidated Chilean government liability data. It operates under the Ministry of Finance.

### 1.1 Estadisticas de la Deuda Publica (Public Debt Statistics)

- **URL**: <https://www.dipres.gob.cl/598/w3-propertyvalue-34954.html>
- **Format**: PDF reports
- **Coverage**: Quarterly reports from 2003 to present (most recent: March 2025)
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Content**: Disaggregated information on financial assets and liabilities of Central Government, Central Bank, and Public Enterprises. Includes:
  - Gross debt stock (internal and external)
  - Debt by currency composition
  - Debt maturity profiles
  - Amortization schedules
  - Treasury assets (sovereign wealth funds)
  - Net debt position
  - Public enterprise debt (separately reported, not consolidated)
  - Fiscal guarantees for public enterprise debt

### 1.2 Informe de Pasivos Contingentes (Contingent Liabilities Report)

- **URL**: <https://www.dipres.gob.cl/598/w3-propertyvalue-16136.html>
- **Format**: PDF reports (annual)
- **Coverage**: Annual reports from 2007 to 2025 (19 reports). DIPRES has compiled this since 2002 but as standalone report since 2007.
- **Frequency**: Annual (published ~December each year)
- **Content**: Fiscal commitments that may or may not become enforceable. Covers:
  - State guarantees for minimum pensions
  - Public works concession guarantees (Ingresos Minimos Garantizados / IMG)
  - Public enterprise debt guarantees
  - Higher education financing guarantees (CAE — Credito con Aval del Estado)
  - Deposit guarantees (bank deposit insurance)
  - Lawsuits against the Treasury (demandas contra el Fisco)
  - Central Bank patrimonial situation
  - CORFO risk coverage funds
  - Obligations with multilateral organizations
  - **2025 total stock**: 10.36% of GDP
  - Valuation methodologies include Monte Carlo simulations and Black-Scholes

### 1.3 Informe de Finanzas Publicas (Public Finance Report — IFP)

- **URL**: Quarterly PDFs at dipres.gob.cl (e.g., <https://www.dipres.gob.cl/598/articles-404483_Informe_PDF.pdf> for Q4 2025)
- **Format**: PDF
- **Coverage**: Quarterly
- **Content**: Comprehensive fiscal report including debt projections, budget execution, fiscal balance, medium-term fiscal outlook. Q4 2025 report projects 2026 gross debt at $151.3 trillion CLP (42.0% of GDP).

### 1.4 Reporte Trimestral de Deuda Bruta del Gobierno Central

- **URL**: <https://www.dipres.gob.cl/598/w3-propertyvalue-15500.html>
- **Format**: PDF
- **Coverage**: Quarterly
- **Content**: Focused quarterly report on Central Government gross debt, including maturity profiles and amortization flows.

### 1.5 Anuario de Estadisticas de las Finanzas Publicas

- **URL**: <https://www.dipres.gob.cl/598/w3-propertyvalue-15407.html>
- **Format**: PDF
- **Content**: Annual yearbook of public finance statistics, consolidating fiscal data.

### 1.6 DIPRES Datos Abiertos (Open Data Portal)

- **URL**: <https://www.dipres.gob.cl/598/w3-propertyvalue-24024.html>
- **Format**: Interactive visualizations with downloadable data
- **Content**: Budget execution, public debt series, fiscal statistics. Includes "Deuda bruta del Gobierno Central" as a downloadable series.
- **Note**: ChileAtiende describes this at <https://www.chileatiende.gob.cl/fichas/112761-portal-de-series-estadisticas-de-dipres>

### 1.7 SIGFE 2.0 (Sistema de Informacion para la Gestion Financiera del Estado)

- **URL**: <https://dipres.gob.cl/590/w3-propertyvalue-24385.html> (Financial Statements)
- **URL**: <https://sigfe.dipres.gob.cl/590/w3-channel.html> (Main portal)
- **Format**: PDF financial statements per institution
- **Coverage**: Annual financial statements from 2018 to 2025 for each central government institution
- **Content**: Balance sheet (assets and liabilities), income statement, cash flow statement, budget situation, changes in net equity. Prepared under NICSP (Chilean IPSAS) standards.

### 1.8 Deuda Publica Section

- **URL**: <https://www.dipres.gob.cl/598/w3-propertyvalue-15501.html>
- **Content**: Central aggregation page for all public debt publications.

## 2. Ministerio de Hacienda (Ministry of Finance) — Oficina de la Deuda Publica

### 2.1 Public Debt Office Main Portal

- **URL**: <https://www.hacienda.cl/areas-de-trabajo/finanzas-internacionales/oficina-de-la-deuda-publica>
- **Content**: Proposes debt strategy, implements bond issuance decisions. Provides:
  - Current debt metrics (as of Sept 2025: gross debt 43.3% of GDP)
  - Bond amortizations schedule (2026: US$7,211 million)
  - Average debt maturity (10.4 years)
  - Credit ratings (A2/A-/A — Moody's/Fitch/S&P)
  - ESG bond program information

### 2.2 Informe de Estadisticas de la Deuda Publica (co-published with DIPRES)

- **URL**: <https://www.hacienda.cl/areas-de-trabajo/finanzas-internacionales/oficina-de-la-deuda-publica/estadisticas>
- **Format**: PDF reports
- **Coverage**: Semi-annual (June and December), historical data from 1991 available in Excel
- **Content**: Detailed debt decomposition: Central Government (Treasury + CORFO), Central Bank debt, public enterprise debt. Covers internal and external debt separately.
- **Most recent**: December 2025 report

### 2.3 Bond Auction Data

- **URL**: Via hacienda.cl/oficina-de-la-deuda-publica
- **Content**: Complete auction calendar, auction results, emission plans (annual), book-building placements
- **Format**: Published on website

### 2.4 ESG/Thematic Bonds

- **URLs**:
  - <https://www.hacienda.cl/areas-de-trabajo/finanzas-internacionales/oficina-de-la-deuda-publica/bonos-esg/bonos-slb>
  - Previous: old.hacienda.cl/oficina-de-la-deuda-publica/documentos-legales/bonos-soberanos.html
- **Content**: Thematic bonds represent ~35% of public debt (~US$44.5 billion since 2019), including green bonds, social bonds, and sustainability-linked bonds (SLB). Legal documents, frameworks, and allocation reports available.

### 2.5 Investor Relations

- **URL**: Via hacienda.cl
- **Content**: Market maker program, ratings history, legal documents, investor presentations

## 3. Banco Central de Chile (Central Bank of Chile)

### 3.1 Base de Datos Estadisticos (BDE — Statistical Database)

- **URL**: <https://si3.bcentral.cl/siete/> (main portal)
- **URL**: <https://si3.bcentral.cl/setgraficos/> (graphical interface)
- **Format**: Interactive web interface with Excel/CSV downloads
- **Frequency**: Updated on the 7th and 23rd of each month (or next business day)
- **Content**: Comprehensive macroeconomic statistics including:
  - External debt series
  - Government financial accounts
  - Monetary aggregates
  - International reserves
  - Interest rates
  - Securities markets

### 3.2 API BDE (Programmatic Access)

- **URL**: <https://si3.bcentral.cl/estadisticas/principal1/web_services/index.htm>
- **Documentation**: <https://si3.bcentral.cl/estadisticas/Principal1/Web_Services/doc_es.htm>
- **Format**: REST API returning JSON
- **Authentication**: Requires free registration (email + password)
- **Endpoints**:
  - `GetSeries` — retrieve time series data by series code
  - `SearchSeries` — catalog of available series by frequency (DAILY, MONTHLY, QUARTERLY, ANNUAL)
- **Base URL**: `https://si3.bcentral.cl/SieteRestWS/SieteRestWS.ashx`
- **Python library**: `bcchapi` (also community SDK at github.com/LautaroParada/bcch-sdk)
- **Series catalog**: Available as downloadable Excel file

### 3.3 Deuda Externa (External Debt)

- **URL**: <https://www.bcentral.cl/areas/estadisticas/deuda-externa>
- **Format**: PDF reports + downloadable data via BDE
- **Content**: External debt by sector (government, central bank, financial corporations, non-financial corporations), by maturity, by instrument type.

### 3.4 Cuentas Nacionales por Sector Institucional (CNSI — National Accounts by Institutional Sector)

- **URL**: <https://www.bcentral.cl/en/areas/statistics/national-accounts/national-accounts-by-institutional-sector>
- **Format**: Excel/CSV via BDE
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Content**: Complete integrated system of accounts including:
  - Current accounts, accumulation accounts, and financial balances
  - Government General sector: gross disposable income, gross savings, net lending/borrowing
  - **Net acquisition of financial assets**
  - **Net liabilities incurred**
  - **Financial balance sheet (assets and liabilities)**
  - Sectors: Households, Non-financial corporations, Financial corporations, Government, Rest of World

### 3.5 Balanza de Pagos y Posicion de Inversion Internacional (BOP & IIP)

- **URL**: <https://www.bcentral.cl/web/banco-central/areas/estadisticas/balanza-de-pagos-y-posicion-de-inversion-internacional>
- **Format**: PDF reports + downloadable data
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Content**: International investment position showing Chile's external assets and liabilities by sector, including government sector external obligations.

### 3.6 Informe de Estabilidad Financiera (Financial Stability Report — FSR)

- **URL**: <https://www.bcentral.cl/en/areas/financial-policy/fsr>
- **Format**: PDF
- **Frequency**: Semi-annual (H1 and H2)
- **Content**: Analysis of fiscal risks, government debt sustainability, sovereign bond market conditions, financial system risks. Contextualizes government debt within broader financial stability framework.

### 3.7 Boletin Estadistico (Statistical Bulletin)

- **URL**: <https://www.bcentral.cl/en/content/-/detalle/boletin-estadistico>
- **Format**: PDF with Excel/CSV download tools
- **Frequency**: Updated on 7th and 23rd of each month
- **Coverage**: Since January 1928
- **Content**: Comprehensive statistics including external debt, international reserves, balance of payments, government financial data.

## 4. Contraloria General de la Republica (Office of the Comptroller General)

### 4.1 Balance del Sector Publico / Cuenta Publica

- **URL**: <https://www.contraloria.cl/> (main portal)
- **URL**: Financial statements accessible via DIPRES/SIGFE portal
- **Format**: PDF reports
- **Content**: As Chile's supreme audit institution, the Contraloria:
  - Carries the General Accounting of the Nation
  - Consolidates financial statements of all public sector entities
  - Publishes the Balance del Sector Publico (Public Sector Balance)
  - Oversees compliance with NICSP (Chilean IPSAS) accounting standards
  - Reports on patrimonial situation (assets, liabilities, net equity) of government entities

### 4.2 NICSP (Normas Internacionales de Contabilidad del Sector Publico)

- **URL**: <http://sistemas.contraloria.cl/portalweb/web/cgr/nicsp1>
- **Content**: Accounting standards governing how government liabilities are measured, recognized, and reported. Resolution No. 16 (2015) established the framework. Includes rules for:
  - Statement of Financial Position (Balance Sheet)
  - Recognition of financial liabilities
  - Contingent liabilities disclosure

### 4.3 Situacion Patrimonial del Sector Municipal

- **Content**: Summaries of municipal operations including assets and liabilities per municipality, prepared according to the current chart of accounts.

## 5. Superintendencia de Pensiones (Pension Superintendence)

### 5.1 Main Statistics Portal

- **URL**: <https://www.spensiones.cl/portal/institucional/594/w3-propertyname-621.html>
- **Format**: PDF reports, Excel/XLS downloads, online databases
- **Content**: Comprehensive pension system statistics including:
  - AFP fund balances and performance
  - Pension payments and beneficiaries
  - Contributor and affiliate data
  - Commission structures
  - Investment portfolios

### 5.2 Pension System Database

- **URL**: <https://www.spensiones.cl/apps/bdp/index.php> (direct database access)
- **Format**: Downloadable databases (CSV-like), with privacy protections (anonymized)
- **Content**: Micro-level pension data for researchers

### 5.3 Bono de Reconocimiento (Recognition Bonds)

- **URL**: <https://ips.gob.cl/fichas/92716> (via IPS — Instituto de Prevision Social)
- **Content**: Recognition bonds are government obligations to workers who transferred from the old pay-as-you-go pension system to the AFP system. As of September 2025, stock was 0.02% of GDP (rapidly declining since 1985 peak).

### 5.4 Ficha Estadistica Previsional (Statistical Pension Factsheet)

- **URL**: <https://www.spensiones.cl/portal/institucional/594/w3-propertyvalue-10121.html>
- **Format**: PDF/Excel
- **Frequency**: Quarterly (includes regional breakdowns)

### 5.5 Antiguo Sistema de Pensiones (Old Pension System)

- **URL**: <https://www.spensiones.cl/portal/institucional/594/w3-propertyvalue-9898.html>
- **Content**: Data on obligations from the old pay-as-you-go system still being serviced by the state through IPS.

### 5.6 Pilar Solidario / PGU (Universal Guaranteed Pension)

- **Content**: State-funded pension supplements representing ongoing fiscal obligations. Statistics available through the SP portal.

## 6. Contingent Liabilities — Detailed Sources

### 6.1 Credito con Aval del Estado (CAE — State-Guaranteed Student Loans)

- **Reporting**: DIPRES Contingent Liabilities Report + dedicated addendum (April 2025)
- **Data source**: INGRESA (<https://portal.ingresa.cl/>)
- **Key metrics**: Total debt ~US$11,900 million as of December 2023 (UF 283 million). Delinquency rate rising: less than 46% of graduated borrowers were current in 2023. State guarantee activations reached ~$270 billion CLP in 2023.
- **New system**: FES (Financiamiento para la Educacion Superior) at <https://fes.mineduc.cl/>

### 6.2 Concesiones de Obras Publicas (Public Works Concessions / PPPs)

- **Reporting**: DIPRES Contingent Liabilities Report
- **Data source**: Direccion General de Concesiones, MOP
- **URL**: <https://concesiones.mop.gob.cl/>
- **Content**: 31 of 50 active concession contracts include minimum guaranteed income (IMG) clauses. Maximum exposure ~US$8 billion in total guaranteed amounts; potential activation ~US$240 million (~3% of total).

### 6.3 Public Enterprise Debt Guarantees

- **Reporting**: DIPRES Public Debt Statistics Report
- **Content**: Fiscal guarantees for public enterprise debt reached 1.6% of GDP (December 2023). Guaranteed entities include State Railways (EFE, 38.6%) and Metro (10.0%). Public enterprise gross debt: 10.1% of GDP ($28.3 trillion CLP).

### 6.4 Deposit Insurance

- **Reporting**: DIPRES Contingent Liabilities Report
- **Content**: State guarantee on bank deposits (up to specified limits per depositor).

### 6.5 Lawsuits Against the Treasury

- **Reporting**: DIPRES Contingent Liabilities Report
- **Content**: Increased from 0.05% to 0.30% of GDP in 2024.

## 7. Additional / Complementary Sources

### 7.1 Consejo Fiscal Autonomo (CFA — Autonomous Fiscal Council)

- **URL**: <https://cfachile.cl/>
- **Statistics portal**: <https://cfachile.cl/estadisticas-fiscales>
- **Historical database**: <https://cfachile.cl/estadisticas-fiscales/bases-de-datos/informacion-historica-anual-de-las-principales-variables-fiscales>
- **Format**: Excel download, interactive visualizations
- **Content**: Curated statistical summary of Central Government fiscal situation including:
  - Gross debt and Treasury assets evolution (2007-2025)
  - Currency composition of gross debt
  - Structural and effective fiscal balances (1990-2025)
  - Historical annual data on all main fiscal variables
- **Role**: Independent oversight body monitoring compliance with fiscal rules (structural balance + prudent debt level of 45% GDP).

### 7.2 Presupuesto Abierto (Open Budget Platform)

- **URL**: <https://presupuestoabierto.gob.cl/>
- **API**: <https://api.presupuestoabierto.gob.cl/about-data>
- **Format**: Interactive visualizations + API
- **Content**: Budget execution data for Central Government. Primarily revenue/expenditure focused but includes debt service payments.

### 7.3 Datos.gob.cl (National Open Data Portal)

- **URL**: <https://datos.gob.cl/>
- **Format**: Various (CSV, Excel, JSON)
- **Content**: 300+ datasets related to monthly budget execution from 2013 onwards.

### 7.4 SINIM (Sistema Nacional de Informacion Municipal)

- **URL**: <https://www.sinim.gov.cl/> (main)
- **URL**: <https://datos.sinim.gov.cl/> (data portal)
- **URL**: <https://www.sinim.gov.cl/desarrollo_local/informe_deudaSF/informacion_informe_deuda.php> (debt reports)
- **Format**: Online database, downloadable data
- **Content**: Municipal-level financial data including:
  - Balance sheet (assets and liabilities) per municipality
  - Quarterly liability reports (Informe de Pasivos Trimestrales)
  - Monthly expenditures
  - Floating debt (deuda flotante)
  - Budget execution
  - Financial statements per NICSP standards

### 7.5 SEP (Sistema de Empresas Publicas)

- **Content**: Financial statements and debt of state-owned enterprises are reported in the DIPRES Public Debt Statistics report as a complementary section.

### 7.6 International Sources (for cross-reference)

- **World Bank**: <https://datos.bancomundial.org/indicador/GC.DOD.TOTL.GD.ZS?locations=CL> (Government debt as % of GDP)
- **IMF**: Government Finance Statistics, Article IV consultations
- **OECD**: Government at a Glance, Economic Surveys
- **S&P Global**: Chilean sovereign bond indices

## Key Metrics Snapshot (Latest Available, ~2025)

- **Central Government Gross Debt**: 41.7% of GDP (2025), projected 42.0% for 2026
- **Prudent Debt Level**: 45% of GDP (CFA recommendation)
- **Contingent Liabilities**: 10.36% of GDP (2024)
- **Public Enterprise Gross Debt**: ~10.1% of GDP
- **CAE Student Loan Exposure**: ~US$11.9 billion total outstanding
- **Concession IMG Maximum Exposure**: ~US$8 billion
- **Recognition Bonds**: 0.02% of GDP (near extinction)
- **Credit Ratings**: A2 (Moody's) / A- (Fitch) / A (S&P)
- **Average Debt Maturity**: 10.4 years
- **Thematic Bonds**: ~35% of total public debt (~US$44.5 billion)
